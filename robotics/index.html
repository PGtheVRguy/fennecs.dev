An important lesson to know is that not all robots are built the same, and some older code may be defunct in later updates. So, in the first video (FRC 0 to Autonomous: #5), they call a "new Spark()" object and "Encoder". We dont run with a base Spark object, but with CanSparkMax, see lines 19-27! Also the encoder options are different. The way motors call followers are also different, now instead of motor groups, you will run something along the lines of "leftMotor.follow(rightMotor)", and then pass the master motor in instead of a motor group!
